# WI-system Use Cases

## 1) Product/Engineering Knowledge Base

Inputs:

- ADRs and RFCs
- incident postmortems
- architecture docs
- meeting transcripts

Outcomes:

- durable architecture pages
- contradiction tracking across decisions
- searchable synthesis for onboarding

## 2) Research Program

Inputs:

- papers and reports
- interview notes
- experimental logs

Outcomes:

- evolving topic pages
- explicit open-question backlog
- cumulative synthesis pages per research question

## 3) Personal Intelligence OS

Inputs:

- journal entries
- learning notes
- health/performance logs

Outcomes:

- long-term pattern visibility
- concept and habit pages
- periodically linted contradictions and stale assumptions

## 4) Team Operations Memory

Inputs:

- support incidents
- customer call notes
- sprint retrospectives

Outcomes:

- operational runbooks that evolve continuously
- less context loss across rotations
- improved continuity during handoffs
