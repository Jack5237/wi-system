# Example: Research Wiki

This is a sample wiki tracking research on a topic over time.

## Use case

A researcher reading papers on renewable energy, tracking:
- Key concepts (Solar, Wind, Grid Storage)
- Papers and their claims
- Contradictions between papers
- Open questions and gaps

## Advantage over just reading PDFs

Instead of 20 disconnected papers, the AI builds:
- Concept pages: Solar, Wind, Grid Storage with unified definitions
- Comparison pages: Solar vs Wind in different contexts
- Contradiction pages: Where papers disagree
- Synthesis pages: What we know and don't know

Over weeks of reading, contradictions get flagged, concepts get refined, and patterns emerge.
